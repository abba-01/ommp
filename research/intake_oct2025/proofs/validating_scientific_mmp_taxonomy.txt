Framing MMP as stepping inside observations
Second-order cybernetics provides the theoretical foundation for positioning MMP as embracing rather than avoiding the observer's role. Key framing: Position MMP as "methodological participation" that transforms the observer-observed relationship into collaborative inquiry. The shift from "observation of participation" in modern ethnography provides academic precedent - Wacquant's "observant participation" calls for researchers to "dive into the stream of action" rather than "watch from the bank."
