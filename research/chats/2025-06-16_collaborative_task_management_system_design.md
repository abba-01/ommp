There is no reason to package media elements for the three modules if they are all the same and they depend upon the component anyway. The best and most logical way to do things is to put them all in a central place that is namespaced appropriately. By storing media in the media folder, this enables templates to override it with template overrides versus being required to modify the files shipped with your extension, causing those customizations to be lost during updates.
