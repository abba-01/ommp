 How Registration Works 
 
 Simple 3-Step Process 
 
 Click \"Sign in with Google\" - You\'ll be redirected to Google\'s secure login page 
 Authorize MMP Access - Review and approve the minimal permissions we request

 The entire process typically takes less than 30 seconds. 
 
 

 
 
 What Information We Access 
 
 Minimal Data Collection 
 When you register with Google, we only request access to: 
 
 Basic Profile Information 
 
 Your name (to personalize your experience) 
 Your email address (for account notifications) 
 Your Google profile picture (optional display) 
 
 
 OpenID - A unique identifier to create your MMP account 
 
 That\'s it. We don\'t request access to your contacts, files, calendar, or any other Google services.

 Frequently Asked Questions 
 
 Do I need a Google account? 
 Currently, yes. Google registration is our primary authentication method. If you don\'t have a Google account, you can create one for free at accounts.google.com. We\'re working on alternative registration methods for future releases. 
 
 
 Can I change my email address later? 
 Your primary email is linked to your Google account. You can add alternative contact emails in your MMP profile settings, but your login will always use your Google account. 
 
 
 What if I want to delete my account? 
 You can delete your MMP account at any time from your account settings. This will remove all your data from our systems. You can also revoke MMP\'s access through your Google account settings without deleting your MMP account. 
 
 
 Is my Google data safe? 
 Absolutely. We use Google\'s official OAuth 2.0 implementation, which means we never see your Google password. We only access the minimal information needed to create your account (name, email, and profile picture). All other Google data remains private.
